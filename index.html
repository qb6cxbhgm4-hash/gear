FAQ
Frequently asked questions
How does RefundOS connect to my Shopify store?
One-click OAuth connection. No access tokens, no developer setup. Click "Connect Store," authorize in Shopify, and we sync your products, orders, and inventory automatically.
How does the AI make refund decisions?
Our engine analyzes order history, customer behavior, product category, return patterns, and order value to recommend approve, deny, or escalate. You set the automation rules.
Can I control what gets automated?
Absolutely. Three modes: Suggest-only (you approve everything), Threshold-based (auto-process under a dollar amount), or Full auto. You can also exclude specific products or categories.
How accurate are the stock predictions?
Our models use your actual sales velocity, seasonality patterns, and supplier lead times. Most merchants see a 47% reduction in stockouts within the first month.
What if my supplier doesn't have an API?
No problem. RefundOS supports CSV/XLSX file uploads, Google Sheets connections, data feed links (XML/CSV), and email-based purchase orders for suppliers without APIs.
Is there a free trial?
Yes, 14 days free on all plans. No credit card required. Full access to all features. Cancel anytime.
Can I connect multiple Shopify stores?
Yes. Growth plan supports up to 3 stores, and Pro plan supports unlimited stores. Each store syncs independently with its own dashboard view.
